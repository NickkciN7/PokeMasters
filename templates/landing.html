PokeMasters

Who’s that Pokemon? Do you know Pidgey from Zubat? Can you spot the difference between Jigglypuff and Wigglypuff? Test your knowledge with PokeMasters!

PokeMasters is a web application built with Python and the PokeAPI to simulate the guessing game featured on the original Pokémon show during the commercial bumpers.

Made By Ramses Salcedo, Minxia Dong, Brandon Pinto, and Dominick DiLeo.
